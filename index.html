Categorical Structures and the Yang-Baxter Equation: A One-Day Workshop in Torino

Welcome to the webpage of our One-Day Workshop in Torino!

About the Workshop

The workshop gathers young researchers who are already outstanding in the international stage, to discuss the applications of category theory in the fields of algebra, algebraic categories, internal structures, the Yang-Baxter equation, braided structures, groupoids, and diagrammatic algebra.

Our Speakers

  • Nadja Egner – UCLouvain
  • Benjamin Morris – ULeeds
  • Silvia Properzi – VUBrussel
  • Mateusz Stroinski – UHamburg

Venue

The workshop will be held at the Department of Mathematics, Aula Lagrange, located in Via Carlo Alberto 10, in the center of Torino.
